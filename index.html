About Me

I'm Alexander G. Perez, a Computer Engineer based in the Philippines, passionate about bridging the gap between hardware and software. With a strong foundation in both disciplines, I specialize in designing embedded systems, developing firmware, and building robust full-stack applications that interact with the physical world.

My journey in computer engineering began during my undergraduate studies, where I worked on microcontroller projects and IoT devices. Since then, I've expanded into areas like embedded C, FPGA design, Linux device drivers, and scalable backend systems. I love solving problems that require a deep understanding of both electrical engineering principles and modern software paradigms.

I believe in creating efficient, low-level code that maximizes hardware potential while ensuring reliable communication between sensors, actuators, and cloud platforms. When I'm not debugging circuits or optimizing algorithms, you can find me contributing to open-source hardware projects, mentoring engineering students, or experimenting with robotics and automation.
